About Me

Hi! My name is Eliana Navejas, I'm located in the downtown Kenosha and I graduated from Gateway of Racine Nail School. I have been doing nails for the past 2 years. I have a diverse portfolio, ranging from new trendy and advanced designs to simplistic and classic designs. Always appreciating the support and love from clients like you!
